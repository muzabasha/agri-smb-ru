About

Dr. Syed Muzamil Basha is a Professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at REVA University, Bangalore, Karnataka, India. He earned his Full time Ph.D. from VIT University, Vellore (Deemed to be University (IoE)) (2016–2019), and has 15 years of teaching and research experience, including 2 years of postdoctoral experience at University of Hail, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (2020–2022).

Dr. Basha is recognized as the 3rd Best Scientist at REVA University (AD Scientific Index 2024) with a VIDWAN Score of 9.5/10.

Dr. Syed Muzamil Basha has served as a reviewer and editorial board member for numerous international journals. He is an editorial member for journals such as the American Journal of Health Research and the Journal of Ubiquitous Computing and Communication Technologies. He also acts as an editorial member for Discover Analytics, the Journal of Innovative Technology Convergence, and Milestone Transactions on Medical Technometrics. Additionally, he is a senior reviewer for prestigious journals like IEEE Transactions on Consumer Electronics, IEEE Internet of Things Journal, and Plos One. He actively participates as an adhoc reviewer for several other journals, including International Journal of Project Management, International Journal of Healthcare Information Systems, and Recent Patents on Computer Science. His reviewer role extends to conference peer reviews, where he serves as session chair and technical program chair for international conferences such as the IDCIoT and WSSE.
